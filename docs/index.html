Alle med

Alle med is an attendance tracking app for teachers on Android. Teachers use it to register student attendance during lessons, field trips, and other activities.

Features

  • Register student attendance (present, absent, late, checked out) with timestamps
  • Organize students into groups
  • Add notes to individual attendance records
  • Generate PDF attendance reports
  • Import student lists from CSV or spreadsheet files
  • All data stored locally in an encrypted database on the device

Google Drive integration

The app offers an optional backup feature using Google Drive. When enabled, the app stores an encrypted copy of the local database in a private app folder in the teacher's Google Drive (drive.appdata scope). This folder is not visible in the user's regular Drive view and is only accessible by this app.

The app does not read, write, or access any other files in the user's Google Drive.

Privacy

The app collects no analytics, telemetry, or personal data beyond what the teacher explicitly enters. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
